WHAT is B2B2C ?

B2B2C is business to business to customer. In short, our B2B2C partners are businesses who sell their own custom sets to customers and we prepare and ship out their sets for them. By partnering with us, they can concentrate on creating and marketing new models and leave all the purchasing and preparation to us. In addition to our inventory of nearly 7 million parts, we have strong ties with many suppliers and great purchasing power. Our vast expertise will allow us to prepare your sets with precision and consistency. Purchasing, generic packaging, printing, we can do it all for you. Get in touch with us and lets us help you grow your business!
